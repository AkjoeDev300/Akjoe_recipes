Coconut Rice Recipe

What Coconut Rice Is.

A savory side dish made with rice, cooked in aromatic coconut milk. It is so rich, luscious and bursting with aromatic coconut flavors. You will be surprised that a Rice recipe this simple has so much flavor.

Ingredients for making Coconut Rice

  • Rice
  • Tomato Stew
  • Coconut Milk
  • Chicken/turkey or any protein of your choice
  • Chilli pepper
  • Salt
  • Onions
  • Thyme
  • Seasoning
  • Vegetables; carrots, green peas , green pepper

Steps to making Coconut Rice

  1. Start by parboiling rice in a pot and cook your meat and liver as well cause you'll need the stock.
  2. Fry the meat
  3. Fry the Vegetables
  4. Add chicken stock, tomato stew and coconut milk in a pot to boil.
  5. Next, add the parboiled rice, spice with some salt and pepper and the fried vegetable
  6. Cover the pot and cook on medium heat. This way, the rice does not burn before the water dries up.
  7. Once the rice is boiled, you can serve.
  8. Serve with some plantain, salad or Moi Moi.
