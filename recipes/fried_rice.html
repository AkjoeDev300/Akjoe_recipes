Fried Rice Recipe

Fried rice

Fried rice is one of the most widely eaten Nigerian food; almost every Nigerian accepts it both at home and in Diaspora. I will show you how I make it.

Fried Rice Ingredients

  • 4 cups of rice.
  • 2 cups of sliced Carrot.
  • 2 cups of sliced Green beans.
  • Half cup of peas
  • 1 cup of sliced Green pepper.
  • 2kg of Chicken.
  • Vegetable oil.{200ml}
  • 1 cup of sliced Onions.
  • Curry powder (coloring spice) {2 Tablespoons}.
  • Salt and pepper to taste

How to cook Fried rice

  1. Set your cooking pot on heat, allow drying, then pour in about 200ml of vegetable oil.
  2. Allow heating, then add the sliced onions, stir for 1-2 minutes.
  3. Then add the chopped carrot and green beans, any of these two can actually go first.
  4. Add the sweet corn, stir.
  5. Meat stock (water from the meat) should follow, add ground pepper (optional), add a teaspoon of salt and, add 1 seasoning cube then stir and taste, you may add more salt.
  6. Add the green peas.
  7. Stir for 1-2 minutes then add cabbage and the curry powder (Curry is a yellowish spice that actually adds the yellowish color to fried rice, add and stir till you are satisfied with the color.)
    You would have a yellowish mixture that would often taste overly spiced, don't worry the rice would balance the taste
  8. If you are satisfied with the taste, then add the green pepper, stir.
  9. Add the almost-done white rice and stir
  10. Stir all together, cover your pot and allow to simmer for 5-7 minutes.
  11. Serve with the fried or fried meat (chicken).

This is how you make Fried rice.
